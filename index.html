About CPSC 319

Where Software Meets the Real World

CPSC 319 is a capstone-style course where teams build software for real organizations. Students work directly with stakeholders and ship production-ready outcomes.

The course focuses on agile delivery, technical communication, and strong engineering habits. The showcase is the public launch.
